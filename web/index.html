sph2wav

Flatten NIST SPHERE files into plain WAV or FLAC — in your browser.

Please don't use this if a real tool can do the job. sph2pipe, ffmpeg, and sox all decode SPHERE. But we know they're old and awkward to install — so if that's in your way, drop your .sph file(s) below and get them back converted. Everything runs locally in this page; your files are never uploaded anywhere.

desphere — clean-room, MIT-licensed, zero-server. PCM, G.711 μ-law/a-law, and embedded-shorten (incl. QLPC & μ-law type-8). Decoding compiled to WebAssembly from the desphere Rust core. source.
